Hygiene and safety

Your service is performed in clean, reassuring conditions.

  • Tools are cleaned and disinfected after every client.
  • Files and bits are replaced or sterilized depending on use.
  • The workstation is sanitized between appointments.
  • A consultation is done first if a nail is damaged, painful or irritated.

Frequently asked questions

Before your appointment

How long does semi-permanent polish last?

It usually lasts two to three weeks depending on your nail type, work routine and home care.

Should I come with bare nails?

If possible, yes. Otherwise, add a removal service so the previous set can be removed properly.

What if one of my nails is broken?

Please mention it before your appointment. We can suggest a repair, strengthening service or gentler option.

Do you accept special events?

Yes, appointments can be arranged for weddings, birthdays, photo sessions or groups by request.
